FAQ

Do you provide insurance?

Not for the MVP. We focus on matching + trust tools first. Owners and walkers agree to Terms and waivers. Later, we may add insurance or an optional paid add-on.

How do payments work?

Start simple: owners pay walkers directly (cash or transfer). The platform may add card payments later.

Are walkers employees?

No. Walkers are independent contractors and agree to such in our Contractor Terms.
